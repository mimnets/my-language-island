# 🇫🇷 Free French Learning Resources for Bengali Speakers

## 📺 YouTube Channels
1. **Learn French with Alexa** - https://www.youtube.com/@learnfrenchwithalexa
   - Clear explanations, beginner to intermediate
   - Great pronunciation guides
   - Free lessons with PDFs

2. **Français avec Pierre** - https://www.youtube.com/@FrancaisAvecPierre
   - Native speaker, slower pace
   - Real conversations with subtitles
   - Good for listening practice

3. **Français Authentique** - https://www.youtube.com/@FrancaisAuthentique
   - Everyday French conversations
   - Focus on practical phrases
   - Business French available

4. **Easy French** - https://www.youtube.com/@EasyFrench
   - Street interviews with subtitles
   - Learn how natives really speak
   - Cultural insights

5. **InnerFrench** - https://www.youtube.com/@InnerFrench
   - Intermediate level
   - Monologues on interesting topics
   - Clear, slow speech

## 🔍 Translation Tools (Check AI Output)
1. **DeepL Translator** - https://www.deepl.com/translator
   - More accurate than Google Translate for French
   - Shows alternative translations

2. **Reverso Context** - https://context.reverso.net/translation/
   - See words/phrases in real sentences
   - Better for context understanding

3. **WordReference** - https://www.wordreference.com/
   - Dictionary with forum discussions
   - Good for nuanced meanings

## 🤖 AI Tips for Better Results
When using Groq AI for translations:
1. **Be specific** - "Translate this business phrase for garment industry: ..."
2. **Ask for alternatives** - "Give 3 possible translations with explanations"
3. **Verify with native sources** - Cross-check with Forvo (pronunciation) and DeepL (translation)
4. **Break it down** - "Translate word by word, then give natural Bengali equivalent"
5. **Use phonetic spelling** - "Give French pronunciation using Bengali script"
